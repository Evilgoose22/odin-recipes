Schnitzel Recipe

I like schnitzel. schnitzels make me happy.

Ingredients:

  • Salt & Pepper
  • 3/4 Cup Flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 1/2 Cups Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Oil for Frying
  • 600g Chicken Breasts

Instructions:

  1. Chicken breast prep: cut in half horizontally to create two steaks.
  2. Sprinkle both sides of meat with salt and pepper.
  3. Place flour in one dish, panko in another and whisk eggs in a bowl.
  4. Coat meat in flour, shake off excess. Dip into egg, shake off excess. Press into breadcrumbs, and turn, pressing to adhere, then transfer to plate. Repeat.
  5. Heat oil in a deep skillet over medium high heat.
  6. Carefully place the schnitzels in the oil and cook for 3 minutes until golden, turn then cook the other side for 3 minutes.
  7. Transfer to a paper tower lined plate to drain excess oil.
