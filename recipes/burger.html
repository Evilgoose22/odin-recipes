Burger Recipe

I like burgers. Burgers make me happy.

Ingredients:

  • Burger Buns
  • Beef Mince
  • Iceberg Lettuce
  • Bacon
  • Jalapenos
  • Red Onions
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Gherkins

Instructions:

  1. Slice, and prepare all of your burger toppings and sauces.
  2. Spread the cut-side of the bun with softened or melted butter and toast the cut side on a skillet or on the grill until golden brown.
  3. Preheat grill to medium heat. Divide ground beef into 4 portions and form patties that are about 1/2" thick. Season patties generously with salt and pepper.
  4. Grill hamburger patties for approximatley 3-5 minutes each side on the grill. Flip burgers when juices are accumulating on top of the burger and you have a good sear. Apply cheese in the last 1-2 minutes of grilling then cover with the lid and cook until melted.
