Mocha Coffee Recipe

I like coffee. Coffee make me happy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup hot brewed coffee
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk

Instructions:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Pour hot coffee into a mug. Stir in cocoa, sugar, and milk.
